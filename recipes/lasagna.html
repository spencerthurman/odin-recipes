Lasagna

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Noodles: 1 box
  • Ragu Meat Sauce: 2 jars
  • Cottage Cheese: 2 containers
  • Mozarella Cheese: 1 pound block
  • Ground Beef: 1 pound
  • Onion: 1 whole onion
  • Garlic: 3-4 cloves
  • salt: to taste
  • Pepper: to taste
  • Italian Seasoning: to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F
  2. Place a layer of noodles along the bottom of a 9x13 dish
  3. Chopped onions and mince garlic
  4. Combine in large pan and cook until brown
  5. add ground beef and cook until brown
  6. Add salt and pepper while meet is cooking
  7. When the meat is thoroughly cooked, add the Ragu sauce and cottage cheese.
  8. Stir mixture and add Italian seasoning to taste.
  9. Pour enough of the mixture into the 9x13 to cover the layer of noodles.
  10. add another layer of noodles and repeat until the dish is full.
  11. once the dish is full, generously apply mozarella cheese to the top of the dish
  12. Wrap in foil and bake for 45 minutes to an hour
